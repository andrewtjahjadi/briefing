## 💱 Markets & Macro

- **INR/USD:** RBI/FBIL reference was **₹94.4735/$ at 1:00pm IST**; Yahoo later showed USD/INR around **₹94.31/$**, so the rupee was **firmer** versus recent ₹94.8–95.1 levels.
- **Equities:** **Nifty 50 closed 24,013.10 (-0.64%)**; **Sensex closed 76,802.90 (-0.78% vs RBI-listed prior 77,409.98)**. Breadth was mixed on Nifty (26 advances / 23 declines / 1 unchanged). Sector tone: **healthcare/pharma led** — Nifty Healthcare **+0.74%**, Nifty Pharma **+0.73%** — while **IT was hit hard (-3.65%)**, with oil & gas **-1.18%**, realty **-1.01%**, autos **-0.61%**.
- **10Y India government bond yield:** RBI’s listed benchmark/proxy **6.94% GS 2036 yield was 6.8397% as of Jun 18**; clean Jun 19 10Y close was **n/a** in public pages checked.
- **RBI repo rate:** **5.25%**; the MPC kept rates unchanged on **5 Jun 2026** and maintained a neutral stance.
- **Oil:** Brent was around **$80.65/bbl**, WTI around **$76.60/bbl**. Crude remains material for India because WPI/fuel pressure is already visible and FX reserves fell sharply this week.

## 📊 Economic Data & Policy

- **CPI:** May retail inflation printed **3.93% YoY**; Reuters poll consensus was about **4.0%**. RBI’s Jun 5 policy document had April CPI at **3.5%** and warned that May fuel-price hikes would add about **36 bps** direct pressure to headline inflation in coming months.
- **WPI:** May wholesale inflation rose to **9.68%** from **8.26%** in April under the revised **2022-23 base-year** series. Fuel & Power inflation was **30.33% YoY**; crude petroleum/natural gas was **+61.51% YoY**.
- **IIP:** Latest April industrial output under the new series was reported at **4.9% YoY**, with manufacturing the support. No fresher IIP print found in the 24–48h window.
- **Trade:** May merchandise exports hit **$45.2B (+18% YoY)**, but merchandise imports rose **22.1% to $73.4B**, widening the goods deficit to **$28.2B**; overall trade deficit widened to **$10.5B**.
- **FX reserves:** India’s reserves reportedly fell **$9.99B to $671.63B** for the week ended **Jun 12**, after being around **$681.6B** the prior week.
- **Policy / fiscal:** PIB/PMO announced PM Modi will release the **23rd PM-KISAN instalment worth ₹18,880 crore** to **9.44 crore+ farmers** during the West Bengal visit. PMO also flagged rollout of PMFBY, AgriStack, National Mission on Natural Farming and PM Dhan-Dhaanya Krishi Yojana in West Bengal.
- **Industrial policy / trade:** India and Uzbekistan agreed to work toward **doubling bilateral trade over three years** and address non-tariff barriers; Commerce Ministry cited India’s FY26 goods/services exports estimate at **$860.09B** and Apr–May FY27 cumulative exports at **$162.69B (+14.66% YoY)**.

## 🏭 Business & Sector News

- **Reliance / Jio:** Reliance said Jio Platforms’ board approved the DRHP for a proposed IPO; the filing is expected to be submitted to SEBI. Jio said the issue would include a fresh issue of up to **270M equity shares** of face value **₹10** each.
- **Telecom / satellites:** Mint reported IPO-bound Jio is weighing a satellite-network push to compete with Starlink — strategically relevant if Jio uses the public-market event to fund next-gen connectivity.
- **IT:** Market weakness was concentrated in IT; Nifty IT fell **3.65%**, consistent with pressure in Infosys/TCS-heavy index moves and weak Asia/risk-off tone.
- **Pharma / healthcare:** Defensive healthcare/pharma led the sector board, with Nifty Healthcare **+0.74%** and Nifty Pharma **+0.73%**.
- **Defense / manufacturing:** Defence Ministry signed a **₹425 crore** contract with Bharat Forge for **12 marine gas turbine generator sets** for the Navy, under Buy Indian, with minimum **60% indigenous content**.
- **Coal / chemicals / energy:** PM Modi will lay the foundation for a **₹25,016 crore** coal-to-ammonium-nitrate project in Lakhanpur, Odisha — India’s first commercial-scale project of this type, using BHEL-developed coal gasification technology via a BHEL–Coal India JV.
