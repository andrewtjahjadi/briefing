## 💱 Markets & Macro
- **INR/USD:** RBI/FBIL showed **₹94.6980 per $1 at 1:00pm IST, Jun 24**; the rupee later **settled around ₹94.66/$**, firmer than the prior **₹94.74/$** close, with Business Standard citing state-bank dollar sales and RBI-linked support.
- **Equities:** **Nifty 50 closed at 24,021.65, +197.55 / +0.83%**; **Sensex closed at 76,991.22, +790.54 / +1.04%**. Sector leadership was risk-on: **Nifty Realty +2.17%, IT +2.05%, Private Bank +1.85%, Bank +1.69%, Financial Services +1.54%**; laggards were **Auto -0.42%** and **Metal -0.40%**.
- **10Y India government bond:** benchmark 10Y yield **fell 6 bps to 6.80%**, a three-month low, after RBI Governor Sanjay Malhotra pushed back on rate-hike speculation and crude eased.
- **RBI repo rate:** **5.25%**, held unchanged at the **Jun 5, 2026** MPC decision; RBI remains in **neutral / wait-and-watch** mode.
- **Oil:** Brent was quoted around **$75.52/bbl vs $77.09/bbl prior** in the bond-market report; lower crude helped rates/equity sentiment and is macro-positive for India’s import bill if sustained. WTI: **n/a** from fetched sources.

## 📊 Economic Data & Policy
- **PMI:** HSBC Flash India **Composite PMI slipped to 57.4 in June from 59.3 in May**; **manufacturing PMI fell to 54.5 from 55.0** and **services PMI fell to a 17-month low of 57.3 from 59.8**. Still expansionary, but demand momentum and confidence cooled.
- **New services output gauge:** MoSPI will launch India’s first **Index of Services Production** on **Jul 14, 2026**, with **FY25 base year**, **60-day lag**, and coverage across trade, transport, banking, insurance, telecom, hotels/restaurants, real estate, professional services and recreation. Trial/experimental indices will initially use GST/admin data.
- **RBI stance:** Governor Malhotra said rate-hike talk is **premature**; RBI is watching **West Asia/crude** and **monsoon** risks, but does not yet see inflation becoming generalized. RBI’s FY27 projections from the June MPC remain **GDP 6.6%** and **inflation 5.1%**.
- **Monsoon / agriculture:** ET’s economy feed continued to flag contingency plans for **315 districts** amid roughly **40–42% cumulative rainfall deficit** headlines; this remains the food-inflation risk to track.
- **Trade policy:** India-US ministerial trade talks ended with a “comprehensive review” but **no clear interim deal yet** ahead of a **Jul 24 US tariff deadline**. Business Standard reported FY26 India exports to the US at **$87.3B, +0.92%**, imports at **$52.9B, +15.95%**, and surplus down to **$34.4B**.
- **External/trade:** ET reported India-China border trade via **Lipulekh Pass** will resume after six years; Commerce Minister Piyush Goyal is heading to the UK ahead of India-UK trade pact rollout; India is still targeting **$100B textile exports by 2030**.
- **Regulation:** SEBI introduced a lighter **NISM Series-XXV-B** certification framework for non-core investment-advisory sales/client-facing staff. ET also reported RBI has introduced a bank model-risk framework requiring controls such as AI “kill switches.”

## 🏭 Business & Sector News
- **Adani:** Adani Power plans **₹2T+ capex over five years** to target **45 GW** capacity; Adani Atomic Energy is targeting **10 GW nuclear capacity by 2035**. Group-level FY26 capex was reported above **₹1.5T**, FY26 revenue **₹2.92T (+7.4% YoY)** and net profit **₹46,376 crore (+13.9%)**.
- **Banks / capital markets:** Government’s **IRFC OFS** non-retail portion was subscribed **1.86x**; Centre plans to exercise the greenshoe option to sell an additional **1%** stake. Axis Bank raised **$800M** via dual-tranche dollar bonds, per Business Standard listing.
- **Tata / consumer:** Tata Motors rose about **5%** after growth guidance; Trent rose **4%** after Noel Tata’s AGM comments, with brokerages citing new brands, store additions and long-term growth ambitions.
- **IT / AI / digital:** ET flagged Indian tech’s Nifty share at a record low on AI worries, even as Nifty IT bounced today. Business Standard listed Yotta planning **$7B** capex this year and EXLService acquiring AI-training firm **iMerit for $310M**.
- **Telecom / payments:** DoT notified new telecom authorization norms to shift operators away from the old licensing regime; BHIM app transaction volumes reportedly tripled over 11 months.
- **Aviation / infra:** Air India said a Delhi-Amritsar flight briefly infringed Pakistan airspace; probe underway. NCR planning headlines pointed to four new cities along RRTS corridors, and Odisha outlined a **₹50,000 crore** maritime infrastructure push.

## 📌 Bottom Line
- India got a relief rally: equities rebounded, bonds rallied and the rupee steadied as crude eased and RBI pushed back on hike fears.
- The macro risk has shifted from pure external shock to **monsoon + food inflation + trade-deal timing**; June PMI says growth is still solid but losing edge.
- Watch Jul 14 services-output data launch and Jul 24 India-US tariff deadline — both can change the macro narrative fast.
