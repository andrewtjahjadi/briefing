📌 Bottom Line for a Fabric/Power BI BI portal

  • GitHub’s Qubot article is the most relevant item. It is basically the pattern a BI portal needs for an AI Assistant POC: a governed context layer, MCP-backed data/query tools, and user-facing entry points that answer analytics questions without forcing everyone into raw KQL/SQL.
  • Fabric Activator + business-event publishing matters for Business Alerts. If BI portal alerts are moving from “dashboard notification” to “workflow trigger,” Activator business events are the Microsoft-native path to make alert outputs routable and auditable.
  • Copilot aicreditsused metrics are worth copying conceptually. For any BI assistant, build cost/adoption telemetry early: per-user usage, per-feature usage, and value proxy next to the AI bill. Otherwise the POC will look magical until finance asks what it costs.
