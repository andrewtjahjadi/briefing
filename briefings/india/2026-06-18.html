🇮🇳 India Daily — Thu Jun 18, 2026

📊 Economic Data & Policy

  • Direct taxes: Net direct-tax collections rose 14.64% YoY to ₹5.21T through Jun 17 in FY27; gross collections rose 12.46% to ₹6.1T.

📌 Bottom Line

India’s setup today is constructive but flow-dependent: lower crude is doing real work for the rupee and equities, while RBI is using the window to rebuild FX buffers. The policy signal is pro-inflow — FCNR/NRE relaxation plus the UK trade pact — but the market still needs durable portfolio inflows before the rupee rally becomes more than a relief move.
