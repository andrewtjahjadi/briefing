🇮🇳 India Daily — Fri Jun 26, 2026

Lower crude supports the rupee, bonds, autos and airlines; PMI cooled while WPI remains energy-driven and Modi’s Seychelles visit is next.

💱 Markets & Macro

  • INR/USD: rupee strengthened about 0.3% in the last traded session to ₹94.3950/$; Indian markets were shut Jun 26 for Muharram, so the latest full market close is Jun 25.
  • Equities: Nifty 50 24,056.00 (+0.14%); Sensex 77,100.47 (+0.14%). Sector tone: Nifty Auto +2.25% led on lower crude; Nifty Metal -1.37% and Nifty IT -0.86% lagged; midcaps fell 1.2%, small-caps were broadly flat.
  • 10Y India G-sec: 6.78% on Jun 25, down 2 bp on the session; Trading Economics notes markets resume Jun 29.
  • RBI repo: 5.25%, held unchanged at the Jun 3–5, 2026 MPC; stance stayed neutral and FY27 GDP forecast was cut to 6.6% from 6.9%.
  • Oil: Brent was quoted around $72–73/bbl after the West Asia risk premium cooled; WTI not verified in this run (n/a).

📊 Economic Data & Policy

  • WPI: May wholesale inflation rose to 9.68% y/y from 8.26% in April, driven by fuel/crude and manufactured chemicals/metals; fuel & power inflation was 30.33%, manufactured products 7.48%.
  • PMI: HSBC flash composite PMI slowed to 57.4 in June from 59.3 in May; manufacturing PMI eased to 54.5 from 55.0, services activity to 57.3 from 59.8. Still expansionary, but demand/hiring momentum cooled.
  • CPI / GDP / IIP / trade / FX reserves: no fresh verified 24–48h print found in the public-source check; n/a rather than carrying an unverified number.
  • RBI / markets policy: bond yields eased as crude fell and investors reduced near-term rate-hike expectations after RBI Governor Sanjay Malhotra said it was premature to discuss hikes.
  • Government / investment signal: PM Modi met Amazon CEO Andy Jassy; PIB said Modi welcomed Amazon’s $48B India investment, framing it as youth-opportunity and foreign-investor confidence.

🏭 Business & Sector News

  • Autos / airlines: lower crude was the cleanest market tailwind; autos led sector gains and InterGlobe Aviation jumped 8.5% in the latest traded session on margin relief expectations.
  • Banks / financials: financials supported the indices, with banks/private lenders up roughly 0.9–1.5% after RBI measures aimed at foreign-currency deposits and flows.
  • Energy: Reuters, via The Hindu, reported middlemen and NIOC have offered Indian refiners Iranian crude at $3–4/bbl discounts after a temporary U.S. sanctions waiver; near-term uptake is constrained by existing supply commitments and unresolved payment channels.
  • Regulation / Reliance ADA: SEBI rejected Anil Ambani/Reliance Infrastructure settlement applications tied to alleged misuse/routing of funds; group denies allegations and says matters are sub judice.
  • IT / AI: NDTV Profit reported Indian IT firms are scaling AI services, with sector AI-services revenue estimated at $10–12B and broad adoption of agentic AI platforms.
